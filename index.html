Who We Are

Rooted in Sri Lanka,
Built to Last

Vextro Lanka (Pvt) Ltd was founded on a belief that construction in Sri Lanka deserves the same precision, material integrity, and design intelligence seen anywhere in the world — with the added richness of local craft and tropical sensibility. We are a multidisciplinary team of architects, engineers, and project managers who guide clients from the first napkin sketch through completion.

We work on residences, commercial buildings, hospitality and institutional projects across the island, always treating each commission as singular.

Sustainability First
Passive cooling, local materials, and low-impact systems are standard, not optional.
Transparency
Open-book budgeting and real-time project tracking. No surprises.
Craftsmanship
We partner with the finest local tradespeople who share our standards.
Lifetime Relationship
Our service extends beyond handover — we remain your trusted building partner.
